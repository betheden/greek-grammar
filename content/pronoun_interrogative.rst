Pronoun interrogative
=====================

Glossary
--------

An **interrogative pronoun** is a part of speech which stands for a noun
and asks a questions (e.g., *what*, *who*, *what kind*, *how many*?;
τίς, ποῖος, ποταπός, πόσους).

**τίς** ὑπέδειξεν ὑμῖν (Mat 3:7)

**who** warned you?
