Mood imperative
===============

Glossary
--------

The **imperative mood** is used in giving a command (e.g. *Eat your
food*).

Λάβετε **φάγετε**, τοῦτό ἐστιν τὸ σῶμά μου. (Mat 26:26)

Take. **Eat**. This is my body.
