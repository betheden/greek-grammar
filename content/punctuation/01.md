# punctuation Glossary
There are four punctuation marks as reflected in the following table.

| Character  | English           | Greek             |
|------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| λόγος.     | period            | period            |
| λόγος,     | comma             | comma             |
| λόγος·     | dot above the line| colon or semiolon |
| λόγος;     | semicolon         | question mark     |
