Negative
========

Glossary
--------

A **negative** is a grammatical feature used to indicate that something
is false (i.e., the opposite of affirmative).

καὶ **μὴ** λυπεῖτε τὸ πνεῦμα τὸ ἅγιον τοῦ θεοῦ (Eph 4:30)

and do **not** grieve the Holy Spirit of God
