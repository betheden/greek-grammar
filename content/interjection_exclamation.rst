Interjection exclamation
========================

Glossary
--------

Interjections are short expressions of emotions (e.g. "woe!", "Amen",
"Hosanna")

::

         **ἀμὴν**   γὰρ  λέγω   ὑμῖν          Matthew 5:18
         **amēn**   gar  legō   hymin
         **truy**  for   I say  to you
    for  **truly**       I say  to you
