Determiner number
=================

Glossary
--------

Determiners are words that identify or quantify a noun. Number
determiners are cardinal numbers which quantify or say how many there
are of something. (for example “\ *five*\ ” books)

.. csv-table:: Example: Matthew 4:18

  εἶδεν δύο ἀδελφούς
  eiden dyo adelphous
