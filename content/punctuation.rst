Punctuation
===========

Glossary
--------

There are four marks as reflected in the following table.

+-------------+----------------------+---------------------+
| Character   | English              | Greek               |
+=============+======================+=====================+
| λόγος.      | period               | period              |
+-------------+----------------------+---------------------+
| λόγος,      | comma                | comma               |
+-------------+----------------------+---------------------+
| λόγος·      | dot above the line   | colon or semiolon   |
+-------------+----------------------+---------------------+
| λόγος;      | semicolon            | question mark       |
+-------------+----------------------+---------------------+
